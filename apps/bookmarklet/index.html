handyman

A guided-tour helper that rides onto any website and walks you through it, step by step — no install, one drag.

1 · Configure

Where handyman is served. Serves /handyman.js and the /api the widget calls. Trailing slashes are trimmed.

2 · Drag to your bookmarks bar

Handyman Drag this button up to your bookmarks bar.

3 · Use it

  1. Open any website you want a tour of.
  2. Click the Handyman bookmark.
  3. The launcher button appears — click it and ask "how do I…?".

Works on most sites. Sites with a strict Content-Security-Policy (GitHub, X/Twitter, and other large apps) block injected scripts and cross-origin API calls, so the bookmarklet won't load there. For those, use the handyman Chrome extension, which relays through its content script and isn't bound by the page's CSP. Voice also needs the proxy reachable over HTTPS in production (http://localhost works from HTTPS pages in Chrome).
